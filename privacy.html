Effective Date: July 2026

LocalLift respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Account profile information
  • Location information used for ride matching, pickup, drop-off, and navigation
  • Ride request and trip history
  • Driver application information, including license, insurance, registration, and vehicle details when applicable
  • Payment-related information processed by secure third-party payment providers
  • Device and app usage information

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage accounts
  • To connect riders with drivers
  • To process ride requests and payments
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve safety, security, and app performance
  • To prevent fraud or misuse
  • To comply with legal requirements

Location Data

LocalLift uses location data to show rider and driver locations, calculate routes, improve pickup accuracy, and support trip tracking. Some features may not work properly without location access.

Payment Information

LocalLift does not store full credit card information. Payments are handled by trusted payment processors.

Sharing Information

We share only the information needed to operate the service, complete rides, process payments, provide support, comply with law, or protect users and the platform.

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect user information. No system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Children

LocalLift is not intended for children under 13. Users must meet the age requirements required by applicable law and platform rules.
